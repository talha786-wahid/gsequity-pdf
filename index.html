Greenstone's Commitment Status Description

Book Building

Conversion

Closing

First Stage DD ("1DD")

  • First meeting held with the investor
  • Investor asked questions relating to the opportunity and/or requested additional material
  • Provided positive feedback in the meeting
  • Provided positive feedback post initial screening of the opportunity
  • Communicated interest in conducting due diligence
  • Requested data room access

Advanced DD ("ADD")

  • Requested / completed a follow-on meeting with the investor
  • Investor broadened the attendees from their side to include senior investment team
  • Investor submitted a DDQ for the fund manager to complete
  • Requested references from other investors or portfolio company's management

Closing Mode ("CLM")

  • Received approval for the investment from the decision maker(s)
  • Issued final set of due diligence questions
  • Provided a soft commitment in writing
  • Requested for a subscription package
  • Performed a site visit

Second Stage DD ("2DD")

  • Accessed the data room and viewed / downloaded documents
  • Allocated internal resources to conduct due diligence
  • Requested bespoke documents for due diligence purposes

IC Pending ("ICP")

  • Investor communicated the intention to present the investment opportunity to the decision maker(s) such as -
    1. Investment committee (IC), or
    2. Principal family member, or
    3. Chairman
  • Asked for or scheduled a site visit

Committed ("COM")

  • Communicated a closing date by which the investor would likely complete their commitment to the opportunity
  • Executed the subscription package
